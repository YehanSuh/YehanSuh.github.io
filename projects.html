Beat Up Cancer

A 2D platformer action game developed using Unity. Fight your way through levels themed around battling cancer cells in a pixel-style world. It has 3 different characters and enemy cancer cells inspired by real cancer cells in the world. With 3 different levels and boss fights, the player will slash the cancer cells with unique skills and attacks. Beat Up Cancer is developed to support the cancer patients and families. Through the experience of beating up cancer without losing, this game encourages them to stand against cancer.
